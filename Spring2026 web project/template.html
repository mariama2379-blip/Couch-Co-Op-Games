AS DUSK FALLS

Description:As Dusk Falls is a narrative thriller that follows two families over thirty years, beginning with a botched robbery in 1998. Player choices shape each character’s fate, creating multiple branching outcomes. The game focuses on themes of betrayal, sacrifice, and resilience, and supports both solo and cooperative play. It released on July 19, 2022, for Xbox Series X|S, Steam, and other platforms.

It Takes Two

Description: It Takes Two is an action adventure game built entirely for split screen CoOp, playable locally or online. It blends mechanics from many genres, with each level introducing abilities tied to the story—like Cody rewinding time or May creating duplicates. Progress requires teamwork, and the game includes numerous minigames to keep the experience varied and playful.

OverCooked 2

Description: Overcooked 2 is a cooperative cooking simulation game where players work together to prepare and serve meals in chaotic, dynamic kitchens.

Split Fiction

Description: Split Fiction is a cooperative action-adventure game developed by Hazelight Studios, where players control two contrasting authors trapped in their own stories, requiring teamwork to escape.

A WAY OUT

Description: A Way Out is a CoOp action adventure game from Hazelight Studios, released on March 23, 2018. Designed for split screen play, it follows two prisoners, Leo and Vincent, as they work together to escape and navigate their intertwined personal stories.

REANIMAL

Description: REANIMAL is a horror adventure game that follows the story of two orphaned siblings on a quest to rescue their friends from a hellish version of their home.
